RoomReady

Client: RoomReady, UAE

E-commerce, Hotel Toiletries


Overview

An e-commerce platform designed to cater to customers in the UAE looking for premium hotel toiletries. Offering products from high-end brands like Oroverde and Marevita, the website allows customers to browse and order toiletries from a variety of categories. With a focus on user experience, seamless functionality, and secure payments, the website delivers an effortless online shopping experience.

Key Features

The website features a comprehensive product listing, categorized by both brand and type for easy navigation. Customers can search for specific products, ensuring a streamlined shopping experience. The platform includes a minimum order amount requirement and integrates the Stripe payment gateway for secure transactions. Shipping is facilitated through a third-party provider, TimeExpress Courier, covering all UAE regions. The website also offers attractive discounts, such as a 10% discount for newsletter subscribers and 20% off on the first order. The site boasts a beautiful, user-friendly theme, responsive design, and SEO optimization. Additionally, it has blog functionality for content marketing and mail integration using GSuite for efficient communication, including newsletters, contact, and order emails.

Tech Stack

Frontend: Custom theme (WordPress), HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Backend: WordPress, WooCommerce

Payment Integration: Stripe

Shipping Integration: TimeExpress Courier (third-party shipping provider)

SEO Optimization: Yoast SEO

Mail Integration: GSuite (for newsletters, contact, and order emails)

Challenges Overcome

One of the challenges was integrating multiple functionalities, including payment gateways, shipping, and product categorization, while ensuring a smooth and intuitive user experience. This was resolved through careful planning and effective integration of Stripe for payments and TimeExpress Courier for shipping. The client also requested a system that would allow for promotional offers, which was seamlessly implemented, offering discounts on first orders and for newsletter subscribers. Another challenge was making the site fully responsive across devices, which was successfully handled by using a mobile-optimized design to ensure excellent usability on smartphones and tablets.
